How WinPrivacy works
01

You search an app

Enter any Windows application name into the search bar. Winny immediately begins cross-referencing our database for a full analysis.

02

We compute the Risk Score

Our algorithm compares declared permissions against actual API behaviour across peer apps to produce a Risk Score (RS) from 0 to 4.

03

You make an informed call

You get a clear verdict — Safe, Normal, Anomaly Detected, or High Risk — plus Winny's plain-language summary.
